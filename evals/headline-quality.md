# Evolve Recipe: Headline Quality

## Artifact
- file: headline.txt
- description: Marketing headline or tagline to optimize

## Scoring
- mode: judge
- direction: higher
- budget: 5
- samples: 3

## Criteria
- Clarity (0.25): The headline clearly communicates what the product/service does
- Memorability (0.25): The headline is catchy and sticks in the reader's mind
- Persuasiveness (0.25): The headline compels the reader to learn more or take action
- Conciseness (0.25): Every word earns its place — no filler or unnecessary qualifiers

## Examples
### "Generic corporate speak" → 15
"Leveraging Synergistic Solutions for Tomorrow's Challenges" — vague buzzwords, no concrete meaning, zero memorability.

### "Decent but forgettable" → 50
"Fast, Reliable Project Management" — clear but generic, could describe any of 100 tools.

### "Strong and memorable" → 85
"Ship twice as fast. Sleep twice as well." — concrete benefit, emotional hook, memorable rhythm.

## Context
The headline should work as a landing page hero headline. Target audience is busy professionals who have seen hundreds of similar products. The headline must differentiate.
